BRIC-NABI
Biomanufacturing Center

The Biofoundry at BRIC-NABI, Mohali, is a state-of-the-art facility advancing innovation in agri-food and bio-based material manufacturing.

Developed in line with India's BioE³ (Biotechnology for Economy, Environment, and Employment) policy, it enables rapid design-build-test-learn cycles, streamlining conventional R&D from ideation to commercialization.
