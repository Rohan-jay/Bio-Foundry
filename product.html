Our Product Portfolio

Natural Saffron Dye

Completely natural saffron-based color dye with petals of Palash. Biomass-free, biocompatible, and suitable for textile applications with vibrant color retention.

Bio-Xylitol

50g → Multi-Kilo

Low-calorie sweetener prevents gum disease, promotes dental health, and stimulates the immune system. Ideal for diabetic-friendly products and oral care applications.

Biofertilizer & Biopesticide from Lignin

Utilizing lignin as a sustainable feedstock for natural crop inputs. Eco-friendly microbial fertilizer and biocide for sustainable soil health and chemical-free pest management.

  • Sustainable agriculture solution
  • Enhanced crop protection

Microbial Bio Pigments

Suitable for soap, textile, and pharmaceutical industries. Food-friendly bio-pigments with high stability. Eco-friendly alternative to chemical dye industry.

  • Clean-label certification

Lignin-Based Food Packaging Film

  • 100% biodegradable materials
  • Food-safe applications

Levulinic Acid
